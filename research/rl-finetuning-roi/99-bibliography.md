## Citation-audit notes

This bibliography compresses references from all chapters in the report. Every entry has been cross-referenced against the corresponding source chapter; entries are merged where the same paper appeared in multiple chapters. Dated price data (token prices, GPU spot prices, ARR figures) carries its access date in the consuming chapter. Vendor blog posts are flagged as Tier 4 and cited as corroborating evidence rather than as load-bearing primary sources.

A small number of entries cited within the source chapters as "venue confirmed; full author list not separately verified" (notably some federated-RLHF and length-bias-mitigation papers in Streams S1b and S4) retain that marker rather than being expanded with unverified author names. Where the synthesis depends on those references, the dependency has been kept conservative.

Three forward-projected references — Anthropic's "An Update on Recent Claude Code Quality Reports" dated 2026-04-23 and a small number of 2026 trade-press URLs — are dated within April 2026 (the research project's "today" reference frame). Final editorial review (Phase 7) should re-verify these URLs immediately before PDF export.
