## 5. Reactions & signals worth noticing

- **The "LangChain Exit" trend.** Production teams quietly migrating from LangChain to raw vendor SDKs (OpenAI Agents SDK / Claude Agent SDK / direct calls); LangGraph survives where stateful loops/checkpoints are needed. *(Ravoid blog; Hacker News; widely cited Q1 2026)*
- **MCP traction shocked even insiders.** Hit **97M monthly downloads** by March 2026 (from ~2M at launch — a 50× jump in 16 months); Anthropic donated to Linux Foundation AAIF in December 2025. Protocol layer is now neutral infrastructure. *(DigitalApplied; Linux Foundation)*
- **BCG framing is biting.** *"Agentic AI is both a $200B opportunity AND a direct threat to technology services."* Picked up across SI competitive decks and earnings calls. *(BCG)*
- **The "agent washing" caveat.** Gartner conceded that legacy RPA and basic automation are being rebranded as "agentic" without substance — and the line is being weaponized by builders against vendor pitches. Gartner also forecasts **40% of agentic AI projects will be cancelled by 2027**. *(Gartner; xpander.ai)*
- **Bain's killer stat.** **80% of GenAI use cases met or exceeded expectations, but only 23% of companies can tie initiatives to measurable revenue or cost.** Single most-cited line of skepticism in the boardroom. *(Bain Tech Report 2025)*
- **Deloitte governance gap.** **75% plan agentic AI in 2 years; only 21% have mature governance.** Drives the move from "does it work" to "can we prove business impact under controlled risk" as the buying criterion. *(Deloitte State of AI 2026)*
- **Multi-agent fragility backlash.** Cognition's mid-2025 essay still being cited; "single-agent + rich tools beats multi-agent swarms" is the consensus in builder circles. Direct hit on hyperscaler "agent mesh" decks. *(Cognition; r/AI_Agents discourse)*
- **Geopolitical signal (treat as weak signal).** Reports — multiple media outlets, April 2026 — that **China blocked Meta's planned acquisition of Manus**, framing the Chinese general AI agent as a national-strategic asset. Cross-border agent platforms may face export / data / ownership restrictions. *(Multiple media reports; no authoritative primary disclosure yet)*
- **Cohere is the cleanest non-hyperscaler vertical-AI play.** Bell Canada (largest commercial customer); STC "North for Telecom"; Aleph Alpha acquisition (April 2026) consolidates German public sector. The non-hyperscaler enterprise stack is real. *(Bell Canada; Arab News; CNBC)*
