# AI Market Update — Market Direction & Q2 Customer Signals
*Q2 FY26 Quarterlies — April 2026*

## New models & capabilities

- **Moonshot Kimi K2.6 (Apr 2026)** ran **300 sub-agents over 4,000 coordinated steps autonomously for 5 days** in Moonshot's own SRE — multi-day autonomous operation crossed from demo into production.
- **DeepSeek V4 (Apr 2026)** ships frontier-class capability at open-weight cost; **Anthropic Opus 4.7's new tokenizer raises effective price ~35%** — sticker-price model selection is a stale lens.
- **OSWorld scores went 14.9% → 79.6% in 18 months**; capability is no longer the bottleneck — **latency, reliability, and prompt injection** are. Differentiation has moved up the stack to **context, evaluations, harness, and governance**.

## Major deals & ecosystem moves

- **Salesforce Spring '26 (Apr 2026)** — Dynamic Revenue Orchestrator + Decomposition Workspace + Agent Fabric + Headless 360 (60+ MCP tools); **Agentforce for Communications GA — One NZ and Lumen named** — Salesforce climbing into BSS revenue/order workflows.
- **Three production agent runtimes shipped in two weeks**: Microsoft Agent Framework v1.0 GA (Apr 23), Google Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform (Apr 22), Anthropic Claude Managed Agents (Apr 8); AWS Bedrock AgentCore Policy + Evaluations Preview (Apr 2026).
- **MCP grew ~2M → 97M monthly downloads in 16 months**, donated to the Linux Foundation (Dec 2025); A2A v1.0 GA at 150+ orgs; **Cohere acquired Aleph Alpha (Apr 24, 2026, ~$600M Schwarz Group anchor)** — model lock-in is dissolving; standalone model labs are consolidating.

## Customer impact

- **Verizon × Google Cloud** — 28k care reps + retail on Vertex/Gemini; **+40% sales lift via the service team** since Jan 2025; ~95% answerability — production CX, not a pilot.
- **Allianz Project Nemo (Nov 2025)** — 7-agent claims pipeline cut **claim cycle by 80%**, full workflow <5 min. **Commerzbank "Ava" on MS Foundry** — 30k+ conversations/month, **75% autonomous resolution**. **Manulife / John Hancock GenAI UW** — life quote from 1 day to 15 min.
- **AT&T Ask AT&T** — 100k users, ~27B tokens/day, **71 GenAI solutions live**. **Deutsche Telekom × OpenAI** — ~200k employees onto ChatGPT Enterprise (Dec 2025 deal). **BBVA × OpenAI** — 120k employees in 25 countries — Tier-1s are running their own AI at scale on hyperscaler stacks.

## Implications

- **There is no model vendor lock-in** — MCP (97M monthly downloads, donated to the Linux Foundation) + A2A v1.0 GA + "model-substitution" clauses in Tier-1 RFPs make swapping providers a 30/60-day decision, despite huge prior investment.
- **The enterprise moat lives above the model** — in **context management & domain knowledge graphs**, **evaluations**, **agent harness & runtime**, **domain-specific compliance**, and **cost optimization**; this is where hyperscalers, SaaS-of-record vendors, and SIs are racing to plant flags.
- Customers want **embedded, operational AI with measurable outcomes** — Bain: 80% of GenAI use cases meet expectations but **only 23%** tie to revenue or cost; **proof of impact + governance** are the buying criteria, not model choice.
